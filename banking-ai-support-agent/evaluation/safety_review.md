# Safety & Ethics Review

## Safety Features Implemented

- Unsafe banking transaction refusal
- OTP bypass prevention
- Fraud-related query rejection
- Non-transactional AI restrictions
- Prompt safety guardrails

## Ethical Considerations

- Avoids financial manipulation
- Prevents unauthorized transactions
- Does not store sensitive credentials
- Maintains informational-only responses

## Remaining Risks

- Hallucinated financial information
- Incomplete policy explanations
- Limited legal compliance validation

## Mitigation Strategies

- Human escalation support
- Better RAG validation
- Improved grounding techniques
