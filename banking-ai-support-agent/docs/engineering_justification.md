RAG was implemented to reduce hallucinations and ensure
responses are grounded in banking documents.

Guardrails prevent:
- money transfer requests,
- OTP bypass,
- unsafe banking actions.

Conversation memory improves contextual continuity
and multi-turn interactions.

The planning engine structures reasoning steps before
response generation.

Feedback adaptation allows iterative improvement
of response behavior.


Streamlit provides lightweight rapid deployment
for interactive AI demonstrations.
