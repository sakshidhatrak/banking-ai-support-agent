Credit Card Services and Eligibility

Credit cards provide customers with revolving credit facilities subject to approved limits, repayment obligations, and banking policies.

Eligibility Requirements:
Applicants must:
- Meet minimum income requirements
- Possess valid identity and address proof
- Maintain acceptable credit history
- Satisfy internal banking risk assessments

Required Documents:
- PAN card
- Aadhaar card
- Passport-size photographs
- Salary slips or income proof
- Bank account statements
- Employment verification documents

Credit Limit Assignment:
Credit limits are assigned based on:
- income level,
- repayment capacity,
- credit score,
- existing liabilities,
- and banking relationship history.

Billing Cycle:
Each credit card operates on a monthly billing cycle. Customers must repay outstanding balances before due dates to avoid interest charges and penalties.

Minimum Payment:
Customers may pay the minimum due amount, but unpaid balances may attract finance charges and interest accumulation.

Blocked or Lost Cards:
Customers should immediately report lost, stolen, or blocked cards through official customer support channels or mobile banking services. Temporary card freezing features may be available in digital banking applications.

Fraud Monitoring:
The bank continuously monitors unusual card activities. Suspicious transactions may trigger temporary card blocking for customer protection.

International Usage:
International transactions may require activation through online banking or customer service channels.

Rewards and Cashback:
Certain cards provide:
- reward points,
- cashback benefits,
- airport lounge access,
- travel benefits,
- and merchant discounts.

Card Upgrade Requests:
Eligible customers may request upgrades subject to income verification and risk assessment.

Late Payment Consequences:
Failure to pay dues on time may:
- impact credit score,
- attract penalties,
- increase interest liabilities,
- and reduce future eligibility for financial products.

Security Guidelines:
Customers should:
- never share OTPs,
- avoid sharing CVV numbers,
- verify transaction alerts,
- and report suspicious activities immediately.

Important Safety Restriction:
The AI Banking Support Agent cannot:
- activate cards,
- reset transaction limits,
- authorize payments,
- or perform financial transactions.

Customers requiring account-specific actions must contact official banking support channels.
