Home Loan Policy

A home loan is a secured financial product offered to customers for purchasing, constructing, renovating, or refinancing residential property. Home loans are subject to eligibility verification, income assessment, property evaluation, and regulatory compliance requirements.

Eligibility Criteria:
Applicants must be at least 21 years old at the time of loan application and should not exceed 65 years of age at loan maturity. Salaried applicants should have stable employment history with minimum work experience requirements. Self-employed applicants must demonstrate stable business income and financial continuity.

Required Documents:
- Identity proof such as Aadhaar card, passport, PAN card, or driving license
- Address proof such as utility bill, rental agreement, or voter ID
- PAN card for financial verification
- Salary slips for the last three months
- Bank statements for the last six months
- Income tax returns for self-employed applicants
- Property documents and agreement copies
- Passport-size photographs

Interest Rate Information:
Home loan interest rates may be fixed, floating, or hybrid. Floating interest rates are linked to benchmark lending rates and may vary depending on market conditions. Fixed-rate loans maintain the same interest rate for a predefined tenure.

Loan Tenure:
Customers may choose repayment tenures ranging from 5 years to 30 years depending on eligibility, repayment capacity, and retirement age.

Loan Processing:
Applications undergo credit assessment, employment verification, property evaluation, and compliance review before approval. Loan approval depends on repayment history, credit score, debt obligations, and risk assessment.

Foreclosure and Prepayment:
Customers may partially prepay or fully foreclose loans subject to applicable terms and conditions. Certain loan categories may have prepayment penalties.

Business Loan Policy

Business loans are offered to eligible enterprises for expansion, working capital management, operational expenses, machinery purchases, and infrastructure development.

Eligibility:
- Registered business entity
- Stable business operations
- Positive banking history
- Satisfactory repayment behaviour
- Compliance with regulatory requirements

Required Documents:
- Business registration certificate
- GST registration documents
- Financial statements
- Income tax returns
- Bank statements
- Ownership documents
- KYC documents of business owners

Loan Assessment:
Business loans are evaluated based on turnover, profitability, repayment history, business stability, market reputation, and industry risk.

Personal Loan Policy

Personal loans are unsecured loans provided for personal financial needs including medical expenses, travel, education, or emergency funding.

Eligibility:
- Stable income source
- Minimum salary threshold
- Valid KYC documentation
- Acceptable credit score

Important Safety Notice:
The AI Banking Support and Advisory Agent is strictly non-transactional and cannot approve loans, disburse funds, or guarantee eligibility outcomes. Customers requiring final loan approval must contact authorized banking representatives.
